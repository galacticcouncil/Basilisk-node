fn slash(
    currency_id: Self::CurrencyId,
    who: &AccountId,
    amount: Self::Balance
) -> Self::Balance

Deduct the balance of who by up to amount.

As much funds up to amount will be deducted as possible. If this is less than amount, then a non-zero excess value will be returned.
